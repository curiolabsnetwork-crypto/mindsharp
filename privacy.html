Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Mendebur Studios

Summary: We collect only your chosen username and game scores to power leaderboards. We do not collect your name, email, or location. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

MindSharp is developed and operated by Mendebur Studios. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at info@mindsharp.co.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username — a nickname you choose voluntarily to appear on the leaderboard
  • No email address, real name, phone number, or password is required or collected

Information Collected Automatically

  • Game scores and statistics (moves, time, difficulty level)
  • Session timestamps
  • Device type and operating system (via Firebase)
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, or browsing history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your scores on global leaderboards
  • To calculate your ranking among other players
  • To improve gameplay balance and user experience
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Game scores are stored using Google Firebase Firestore. By using MindSharp, your score data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Username data is also stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any other third party.

5. Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are retained indefinitely to maintain competitive rankings. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@mindsharp.co.

6. Children's Privacy

MindSharp does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request removal of your username and scores
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, email: info@mindsharp.co

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of MindSharp after changes constitutes your acceptance.
