🗑️ Delete My Data

Mendebur Studios  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. You can request deletion of all data associated with your username and game scores at any time. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Option 1 — Delete Scores Directly In the App

You can instantly remove your scores from the leaderboard without contacting us:

1
Open MindSharp Go to mindsharp.co or open the app
2
Tap "🏆 Scores" From the main menu, open the High Scores screen
3
Select difficulty tab Choose Classic, Hard, or Extreme
4
Tap "🗑 Clear Scores" Confirm the dialog — your scores for that category are permanently deleted from the leaderboard

Option 2 — Submit a Deletion Request by Email

To request full deletion of all data associated with your username across all categories, send us an email:

Send your deletion request to:

✉️ [email protected]

Please include your in-game username in the email. No other personal information is required.

What Data Is Deleted vs. Kept

Data Type Storage On Deletion Request
Username (leaderboard name) Firebase Firestore + Local device Deleted
Game scores (moves, time, score) Firebase Firestore Deleted
Local stats & streak (on your device) Browser localStorage Deleted on device clear
Anonymized aggregate analytics Firebase May be retained (no personal link)

Deletion Timeline

  • In-app deletion: Immediate — scores removed instantly from leaderboard
  • Email request: Processed within 7 business days
  • Firebase backup copies: Purged within 30 days
